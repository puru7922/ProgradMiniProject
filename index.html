Personal Care

Our Care Managers use our unrivaled research and connection with our client's family to develop a plan that will give a sense of stability, satisfaction, support, and peace of mind. We have competent personal care specialists who are committed to supporting physically challenged and Aged persons in their everyday lives.

Respite Care

Our Respite care offers a safe haven and comfortable place for your loved one while you’re away. Our trained providers can sit and hold tangible conversations with someone who’s disabled or ill.
We may also help your loved one with their:
1. Bathe
2. Dressing
3. Walks
4. Eat and Drink
5. Exercise
6. Outdoor Enjoyment
7. In-house movement

Post Surgery Care

Our Postoperative care commences after your loved one just had a surgical procedure. Our Trained Team will help your Loved One with pain management and wound care. We are also available to prepare you ahead of time by getting involved in the pre-surgery and post-surgery experience. The attainment and satisfaction that comes with helping others is the drive for our Team.

Grocery Pickup Care

Our Team can also assist with grocery pickup and deliveries. We put your Elderly first with our organized grocery pickup support program.

Companionship

Our Companionship program focuses on providing assistance and friendship to older adults or ill loved ones who have difficulty with daily living tasks, such as shopping or paying bills. We also can sit with and hold meaningful conversations. putting in cognizance the interests of your loved ones.

Elder Care

As we get older we want to age in the comfort of our homes sometimes and this is why we have a Team of seasoned caregivers who are devoted to providing Eldercare to your loved ones. Assisting with basic day-to-day activities like dressing, housekeep, and tasks to make your loved ones feel homely and comfortable.

To & From Doctor's Appointment

Our team will convey your loved ones to their scheduled doctor's appointments in the most comfortable transit possible, we will also make sure of their return home without stress.

Light House Keeping

We have trained housekeeping staff that is capable of keeping the house well organized and in the perfect home condition. We understand that housekeeping is a major part of the home and this helps keeps your loved ones in a good mood.

We’re not a helping hand. We are the expert hand!

It can be quite overwhelming for the family of an elderly patient to deal with the special needs of their ailing loved one. Having a proficient, qualified caretaker at home, can ease your worries and provide the much-needed medical attention and care to the elder. Once you book our care services for special procedures or routine round-the-clock support, our caretakers assesses the medical requirements of the patient.

We’ll steer you to recovery!

Our caretakers’ attentive care can make recovery a comfortable journey. The skill and experience they bring to the table ensure minimum pain and discomfort to the elderly patient, which helps in ‘normalizing’ a seemingly stressful health situation. We very well understand how emotionally taxing it can be for an elder to deal with the loss of mobility or bodily functions. Which is why our caretaker team will handhold him/her to recovery using their medical expertise and continued patience.
